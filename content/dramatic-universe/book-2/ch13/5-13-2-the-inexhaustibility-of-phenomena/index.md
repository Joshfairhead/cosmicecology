Representation of experience by diagram of unity, function, being, and will—human thought confined to examining pairs among these four fundamental categories—natural philosophy primarily concerned with understanding function—unity conceptualized as self-consistency inherent in natural order—universal laws providing omnipresent structural framework—the stratification of existence.

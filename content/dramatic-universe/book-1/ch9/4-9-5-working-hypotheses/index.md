Hypotheses and philosophical systems and summarized statements within phenomenology—Husserl's approach—historical scientific examples: Kepler and Copernicus, Faraday and Clerk Maxwell, Bell and Green in physics—Balmer and Bohr in chemistry/physics—Mendel and Weismann, van Beneden and Platner in biology—every working hypothesis relates to existence as well as to mechanism.

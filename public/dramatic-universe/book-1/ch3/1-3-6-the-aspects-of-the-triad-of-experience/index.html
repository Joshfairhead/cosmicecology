1.3.6 The Aspects of the Triad of Experience

Function, being, and will operate across cosmic, subjective, and objective aspects—knowledge the subjective aspect, behaviour the objective—consciousness represents the subjective dimension while materiality represents the objective—consciousness universal but not in sense of panpsychism—cosmic aspect termed "authorizmos"—law as objective manifestation and understanding as subjective counterpart—combinations of these primary elements give rise to the diversity of experience.
